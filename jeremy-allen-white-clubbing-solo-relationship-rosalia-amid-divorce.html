Jeremy Allen White is arguably at the height of his career thanks to the success of The Bear as well as his newly viral Calvin Klein campaign. And it looked like he was celebrating the other night when paparazzi spotted Jeremy at a strip club without his new girlfriend Rosalia in tow.

As per photos published by TMZ, Jeremy was spotted at Honey’s – an LGBTQ-friendly bar in Los Angeles’ Los Felix neighbourhood – around 10:30 p.m. on Saturday night. He was seen socializing with people at the venue and even getting close to a few of the dancers, though it otherwise seemed like a tame evening for the actor.

Jeremy Partied Hard Without His New Girlfriend Rosalia

The Shameless alum debuted his relationship with Rosalia last month as he continues to navigate his divorce from Addison Timlin, with whom he shares two young daughters.

“Rosalia and Jeremy Allen White have recently started dating,” a source told Us Weekly in December. “They started out as just friends, but things have turned romantic recently.”

The new couple have been photographed on multiple occasions getting PDA-heavy in public, confirming their relationship status. Nonetheless, Jeremy and Rosalia both remain tight-lipped about their romance, choosing not to say anything in the media or on social media. Jeremy was previously seen kissing Ashley Moore sooner after his estranged wife filed for divorce.

Jeremy’s Wife Allegedly Divorced Him Because Of His Drinking

In October, a report said that Addison chose to end her marriage to Jeremy after his drinking became a problem.

“Addison’s not getting into details, but she’s seen some red flags when Jeremy has a few too many, and she doesn’t want him drinking or drunk around the children,” a source told Life and Style . “She’s just being a cautious mom.”

Their current custody agreement stipulates that Jeremy must undergo alcohol testing before seeing their girls. Apparently, the actor isn’t challenging the court’s request. “Jeremy will do whatever it takes to abide by the custody agreement,” the source said.

He allegedly also has to wear a Soberlink device when his daughters are with him, which will ensure Jeremy stays sober if they’re in his care. The report says the actor gets visitation with his daughters multiple times a week.
