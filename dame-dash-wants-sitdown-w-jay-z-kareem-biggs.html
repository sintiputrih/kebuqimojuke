Dame Dash Wants Sitdown w/ Jay-Z &amp; Kareem Biggs

 Posted on July 13, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  184 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Dame Dash wants to sitdown and talk with his fellow Roc-A-Fella founders, Jay-Z and Kareem Biggs, to settle a recent lawsuit.

“Like I said, I don’t have to ask to sell what I own.. #AskTheJudge #StayTuned @dashnftgallery and let’s be clear, it’s #jayz and @biggsburke that are suing me…: he wrote.

“My offer still stands, let’s get in a room like the old days and sit and talk like men. I don’t need a lawyer to talk for me.. I can’t believe this shit, I feel like I’m in the twilight zone”

The pair sued him after he tried to sell a Reasonable Doubt. 

“It’s corny and I think it’s embarrassing. And now I’m in a fight I don’t want to be in when I’m doing other sh*t,” Dash said to HHDX about the lawsuit. “All I want to do with is sell my interests and do what I got to do. Instead, I got to be called a liar, I got to have some white man [Alex Spiro] calling me a thief, somebody trying to tell me how to run my business.”
