Kubota L2850 Compact Utility Tractor is equipped with a Kubota V1702-DI-A diesel 4-cylinder liquid-cooled 105.7 ci [1.7 L] engine, which produces 34.0 hp [25.4 kW] power at rated rpm 2600.

Kubota L2850 Compact Utility Tractor featured a Kubota 1.7L 4-cyl diesel engine, 8 forward and 7 reverse gears, 32.8 Qts [31.0 L] oil capacity, 2,425 lbs [1099 kg] Rear lift (at ends), 4×2 2WD and differential wet disc brakes.

Kubota L2850 Compact Utility Tractor Attachments include a mowing deck, front-end loader, and backhoe
