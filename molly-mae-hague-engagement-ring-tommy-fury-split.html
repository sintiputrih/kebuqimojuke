Molly Mae Hague looks downcast but puts 600k engagement ring back ON despite Tommy Fury split rumo

 Posted on April 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  403 words  |  Larita Shotwell

MOLLY-MAE Hague posed with her £600k engagement ring despite sparking rumours of a split with Tommy Fury.

Molly headed out to a posh restaurant on Sunday and sat in a leather armchair with her huge sparkler on display.

The YouTube and Love Island star, 24, wore a cream off-the-shoulder jumper with black jeans and crossed her arms over her lap.

She failed to raise a smile, but appeared to show a sign of unity with Tommy, 24, as she flashed her left hand.

Molly wrote beside her photos: "Slow pace Sundays."

Many of her fans commented on the post, with one speculating: "Hope you are ok @mollymae. Your eyes tell a story which none of us know. But sending lots of love."

Another concerned fan said: "Hope you start to feel yourself again very soon."

Last month, the couple’s relationship came under scrutiny after Tommy was seen in a club with other partygoers.

Molly then took a break from Instagram after the former Love Island star was filmed dancing alongside rapper Chris Brown and interacting with an unknown female, whilst partying in the UAE.

Tommy was spotted partying without Molly again last week as he posed alongside Married At First Sight star Laura Vaughan, 34.

The pair are signed to the same talent agency, Alpha Talent Group, who were holding their annual Christmas party.

Molly was recently pictured without her pricey engagement ring for a PrettyLittleThing launch.

She also kept fans guessing by hiding her hands with the sleeves of her jumper.

Molly – who met Tommy on Love Island in 2019 – has made no secret about how much she dislikes her fiancé drinking and partying.

She wrote in her book, Becoming Molly Mae: "The only major arguments Tommy and I have had have been to do with him drinking.

"Which he only does a few times a year. He won’t even do anything wrong; I’ll just lose it at him because I hate it.”

Meanwhile, Paris Fury - who has been married to Tommy's older brother Tyson since 2008 - has stepped in and shared some advice with Molly.
