Elder Joe Ingram Car Accident

Elder Joe Ingram’s life was tragically cut short in a car accident that occurred on November 14, 2023. While on his way to catch a flight to South Africa for a COGIC conference, his car collided with a truck that ran a red light in Memphis, Tennessee. The incident took place near the intersection of Lamar Avenue and Airways Boulevard. Despite the efforts of emergency responders, Elder Ingram, who was 62 years old, was pronounced dead at the scene.
