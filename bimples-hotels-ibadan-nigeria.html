BIMPLES HOTELS, Ibadan, Nigeria

 Posted on May 6, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  182 words  |  Martina Birk

BIMPLES HOTELS is a budget hotel located at NO. 2, Agbaakin Street, Freedom Road Idi Ape, Ibadan, Oyo, Nigeria. The hotel offers affordable accommodation to guests and is only 2.5 km from Agodi Gardens

The nearest airport is Ibadan Airport, 25 minutes drive away.
Guests will enjoy the serene environment

Each room at BIMPLES HOTELS has a spacious bathroom, king-sized bed, cable connected TV set, refrigerator, bedding items and air conditioner.

Also available at the hotel are free WiFi, a restaurant, free parking and a bar.

There is an on site restaurant available at the restaurant

Services available include 24-hour front desk, Concierge service, Luggage storage, Express check-in/check-out, Cleaning services, Daily maid service, Ironing service, Dry cleaning/Laundry service and room service

In addition, guests can also enjoy airport shuttle service(additional charges), ironing service(additional services) etc on request.
