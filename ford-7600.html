Ford 7600 Utility Tractor is equipped with a Ford turbocharged diesel 4-cylinder liquid-cooled 256 ci [4.2 L] engine, which delivers 96.7 hp [72.1 kW] power at a rated rpm 2100.

Ford 7600 Utility Tractor featured a Ford 4.2L 4-cyl diesel and Ford 4.4L 4-cyl diesel engine, 4×2 2WD/4×4 MFWD 4WD, 4435 lbs [2011 kg] Rear lift, 5580 to 10760 pounds weight, 9 qts [8.5 L] engine oil capacity, 16 forward and 4 reverse gears.

Ford 7600 Utility tractor attachments include Ford 772, Ford 772QT, and Ford 774 front-end loader.
