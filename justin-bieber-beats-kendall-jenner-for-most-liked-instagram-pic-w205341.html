Justin Bieber Beats Kendall Jenner for Most-Liked Instagram Pic

❥

A post shared by Kendall (@kendalljenner) on May 25, 2015 at 3:51pm PDT

Broken hearted! After an 11-month reign, Kendall Jenner's heart-shaped hair picture isn't the most-liked photo on Instagram any longer.

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star, 20, won the title in June 2015 (and unseated her older sister Kim Kardashian's wedding pic with husband Kanye West). In the image, Jenner lays down on the floor in a white floral dress with her eyes closed and her hair arranged in seven hearts around her head. "❥," she captioned the photo.

"[A picture] has to be organic," she explained to Vogue of her social media strategy in March. To that effect, her feed is comprised of moments including a touristy pic of the Colosseum in Rome, her muse Cindy Crawford posing topless and a still of Spongebob Squarepants.

Feels

A post shared by Justin Bieber (@justinbieber) on Mar 19, 2016 at 4:07pm PDT

The new master of "likes" is none other than Jenner's friend, Justin Bieber, 22. His grainy throwback shot of himself and Selena Gomez kissing, posted on March 19, has now overtaken the Estée Lauder brand ambassador's whimsical hairstyle by over 7,000 double-taps.
