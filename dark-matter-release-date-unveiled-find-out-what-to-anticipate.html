Blake Crouch’s planned science fiction television series Dark Matter is based on his same-titled novel from 2016. The goal of this article is to provide you with all the essential information you require regarding Dark Matter, including its release date, cast, plot, and much more. Internet users have been guessing about the movie’s release date and seeking information about it.

Dark Matter Release Date

Dark Matter is set to debut on Apple TV+ on Wednesday May 8, for fans who were hopeful to see it show up on the 2024 TV premiere schedule are getting there wish.

According to Apple TV+’s release, the first two episodes of the series will premiere on May 8. Following that, one episode will be available for streaming every week on Wednesday until June 26.

Cast of Dark Matter

• Jason Dessen had by Joel Edgerton

• Daniela Dessen, played by Jennifer Connelly

• Amanda, played by Alice Braga

• Ryan, played by Jimmi Simpson

• Charlie Dessen played by Oakes Fegley

• Leighton, played by Dayo Okeniyi

• Blaire, played by Amanda Brugel

Dark Matter Storyline 

Dark Matter, regarded as one of the best science fiction books of the decade, tells the tale of the path less traveled. The show will center on Joel Edgerton’s character, Jason Dessen, a scientist, professor, and family guy who gets kidnapped into a different version of his life one night while strolling home through the streets of Chicago.

When he tries to return to reality amid the surreal landscape of lives he could have had, Wonder swiftly transforms into a nightmare. He sets out on a terrible adventure through this maze of realities in an attempt to return to his actual family and save them from the most terrifying and unbeatable enemy imaginable: himself.
