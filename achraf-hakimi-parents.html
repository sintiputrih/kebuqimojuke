The vital ingredients behind his journey to the top

Achraf Hakimi, is a footballer of Moroccan origin. He spent his childhood in Getafe. Hakimi grew up in a very modest family. 

Achraf Hakimi father is a street vendor, and his mother is a cleaner at building sites. He has a sister, Ouidad, and a brother, Nabil.

Hakimi was attracted to football from an early age. He was introduced to Club Deportivo Colonia Ofigevi. One of the footballers he most admires and draws inspiration from is Marcelo.

From the age of 7, Hakimi joined the Real Madrid academy. In 2015–2016, he played UEFA Youth League matches and reached the semi-finals.

A year later, he played with second-division club Real Madrid Castilla.

His good performances lead Zinédine Zidane to ask him several times to participate in the internships and training sessions of the Real first division team. 

Hakimi took part in the 2018 World Cup with the Morocco team. That same year, he left to play in Germany following a loan to Borussia Dortmund. In 2019, then aged 21, the young footballer was named one of the 50 best young players in the world. 

In 2020, he joined the Italian professional team Inter Milan with which he played for a year before joining Paris Saint-Germain. In 2022, he participated in the World Cup in Qatar with the Morocco team and played a match against the France team and his friend Kylian Mbappé. 

On July 6, 2021, he signed a contract with Paris Saint-Germain for a sum of 60 million euros with 11 million in bonuses.
