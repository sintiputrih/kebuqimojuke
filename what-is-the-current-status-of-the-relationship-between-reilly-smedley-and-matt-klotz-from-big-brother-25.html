Who is Matt Klotz?

Matt Klotz is a contestant who became the runner-up on Big Brother 25. Born on May 24, 1996, in Cameron Park, CA, and Baton Rouge, LA, Matt is 27 years old. Big Brother 25

Big Brother 25 is the 25th season of the American reality TV show Big Brother. The season premiered on August 2, 2023, on CBS in the United States. Hosted by Julie Chen Moonves, the show features 17 contestants called HouseGuests, who live together in a specially designed house under constant camera surveillance. They have no contact with the outside world and compete to win a grand prize of $750,000.

Throughout the season, HouseGuests participate in various competitions, including the Head of Household competition, where the winner gains immunity and nominates two others for eviction. The Power of Veto competition allows some HouseGuests to potentially change the nominations.

The season consists of 42 episodes and concluded on November 9, 2023, with Jagateshwar “Jag” Bains winning the grand prize and Matt Klotz as the runner-up. The format of Big Brother involves strategic gameplay, alliances, and weekly evictions, creating a dynamic and competitive environment.
