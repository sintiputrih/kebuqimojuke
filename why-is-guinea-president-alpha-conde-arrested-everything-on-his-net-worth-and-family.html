Alpha Conde is the president of Guinea. He had held the position since December 21, 2010, but has recently been arrested and has been making headlines across the internet, and is trending on the major social media platforms.

The politician started his political career back in the year 1993 as a presidential candidate but lost to  President Lansana Conté. He was also an active supporter of the National Union of Higher Education when he left for France at the age of 15 years old.

Let us learn more about Alpha Conde and take a closer look at his net worth and family background.

What Is Alpha Conde Net Worth?

Alpha Conde might have a net worth of over $1 million as the president of Guinea.

However, he has not revealed anything about his actual net worth to the general public or on the web yet.

The president had his salary cut down from 20 million Guinean francs to 15 million Guinean francs according to PayCheck.

As per today’s exchange rates, his 15 million Guinean francs equals $153.29 per month.

Who Is Alpha Conde Wife? Is He Married?

Alpha Conde is married to his wife Djene Kaba Condé.

Djene has been the first lady of Guinea since 2010 after her husband became the president of the country.

However, Kaba is not the only wife Alpha has married in his life. He has been married three times until now.

His first wife was  Mina Kone. Although their marriage date and married life have not come to the surface yet they parted their ways in the year 1973.

Then Alpha married his second wife named  Nènè Kanny Diallo. He has not revealed his second marriage date as well on the web but he again got divorced with his second wife also in the year 1998.

As of now, he is happily married to his third wife Djene Kaba Conde. His wife must be very worried amid his recent arrest.

What Is Alpha Conde Family Background?

Alpha Conde comes from African family background.

He was born to his father Mohamed Condé and mother Saran Camara in  Boke in Lower Guinea. His parents come from Burkina Faso.

The politician also has one child of his own named Alpha Mohammed Condé. However, much about him has not come to the surface yet.

Why Is Alpha Conde Arrested?

Alpha Conde has been arrested by the Guinean special forces according to France24.

In a short broadcast, the special forces said they have arrested the president after the country turned into turmoil after he was elected for the third time.

His third election was the major reason behind the unrest inside of the country as he made amendments to the constitution and the opposition party was not so happy with the decision according to his Wikipedia profile.
