Biography:

Angela Sarafyan was born on June 30, 1983 in Yerevan, Armenia. At age four, she moved to the United States with her parents. In 2003, she made her television debut as a guest star in Buffy the Vampire Slayer.

She has since appeared in such television shows as South of Nowhere, In Plain Sight, The Mentalist, The Good Guys, Criminal Minds, and Law & Order: Special Victims Unit.

In 2008, she made her motion picture debut in the film The Informers. She then gained recognition for her role as Tia in The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn – Part 2.
