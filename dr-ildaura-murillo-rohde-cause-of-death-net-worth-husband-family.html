Dr. Ildaura Murillo-Rohde Net Worth: Meet her on Wikipedia

Dr. Ildaura Murillo-Rohde had great figures of net worth, however, her work had the most value.

She started her nursing career at the age of 25 after moving to Texas from Panama. Starting her career as a helper in local hospitals, she later pursued it academically.

Due to the deficiency of nurses in the Hispanic community, she made a decision to study psychiatric nursing. In 1953, she graduated from New York’s Columbia University.

Also, Ildaura was the first Hispanic nurse to earn a Ph.D. degree from New York University. She was the founder of the National Association of Hispanic Nurses.
