Brynn Rumfallo Wiki Biography

Brynn Erin Ashlee Rumfallo was born on the 1st July 2003, in Phoenix, Arizona USA, and is a professional dancer and a model who is probably best known for being a former member of the child dance crew named Fresh Faces with which she appeared on the television reality show America’s Got Talent. She is also widely recognized for starring in “Dance Moms” reality series.
