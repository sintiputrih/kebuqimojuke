All About Patrick Mahomes Parents: Pat Mahomes Sr. and Randi Martin

Pat Mahomes Sr. and Randi Martin, the parents of NFL Superstar quarterback Patrick Mahomes, have had a significant impact on his life. The way they balance their relationship and what each of them contributes make Patrick who he is in his football career.

Pat Mahomes Sr.

Pat Mahomes Sr. was born in Lindale, Texas, on August 9, 1970. As an athlete in football, basketball, and baseball for Lindale High School, Allen was an all-state player during his high school years. He would later move on to a Major League Baseball (MLB) pitching career. He played professionally from 1992 to 2003 with the Minnesota Twins, Boston Red Sox, New York Mets (twice), Texas Rangers, Chicago Cubs, and Pittsburgh Pirates.

Randi Martin

Randi Martin was born on January 18, 1976, in Tyler, Texas, and she has played a key role throughout Patrick's life. She spent much of them growing up in small East Texas.

They married in the late '90s and shared two sons, Patrick and Jackson, before officially divorcing in 2006. Even though the couple had to overcome several hurdles during their marriage, they still maintain a healthy co-parenting relationship and frequently get together again for Patrick's games and key events.

The Mahomes Family Dynamic

While the Mahomes family faced challenges, Patrick's success is partly due to his complex dynamics with the people he loves most. Having Randi and Pat as a presence in his life for the entirety of it has only added to Patrick's development as an athlete through their unwavering support. The family's tightness and insistence on keeping their values as top priorities have been a part of Patrick's path to NFL stardom.

Pat Mahomes Sr. and Randi Martin influenced Patrick Mahomes's life, creating a domino effect on his career. The Mahomes family tale proves not only that parents can significantly influence their children's lives, between the lines and outside of it, but also how.
