Lauren Manzo is an American reality television personality. She is better known as the daughter of former Real Housewives of New Jersey star Caroline Laurita Manzo.

Personal Life : Parents, Family and Education

  • Lauren was born in April 1988 in New Jersey.
  • She was raised in Franklin Lakes, New Jersey, alongside her older brother Albie Manzo & her younger brother Chris Manzo.
  • Through her father, Albert Manzo, she is rumored to have been the granddaughter of a notorious mob enforcer.
  • Her paternal grandfather, Albert ‘Tiny Manzo, was well-known for being involved with the Mafia. Still, their family has tried to downplay this connection & has advised people not to believe everything they read.
  • Her father & her mother, Caroline Manzo, run “The Brownstone” in Paterson, New Jersey. It’s an events & catering establishment. Lauren worked there in the summers while growing up.

Lauren Manzo Pregnant and Marriage :

  • Lauren got engaged to long-time boyfriend Vito Scalia in November 2013.
  • They married on 18th July 2015 at a local church in a traditional catholic ceremony. The reception was held at her parent’s venue, The Brownstone.
  • Their wedding was a big social media affair as they encouraged their friends & family to post throughout with the hashtag #HappyWifeDeliLife.
  • She made no secret of her desire to start a family from the time she was married, and in September of 2016, she was finally able to announce her pregnancy.
  • She gave birth to their first child, a daughter named Marchesa Scalia, on 24 February 2017.
