Dawn French shares incredibly rare photo of daughter Billie

 Posted on March 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  372 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

The French and Saunders star adopted Billie in 1991

    Dawn French keeps much of her private life under wraps, but on Monday, the star treated her followers to an incredibly rare photo of her daughter, Billie.

    Taking to Instagram Stories, the beloved comedian shared a snapshot of her daughter smiling for the camera. In a bid to protect her privacy, Dawn, 65, masked her loved one's face with a sprig of spring blossom.

    While Billie's face was hidden, the 32-year-old looked stylish in a forest green hoodie which she wore with the hood up.

    Dawn paid tribute to her daughter with a touching caption. Alongside the snap, she sweetly penned: "My beautiful girl."

    The TV star and her ex-husband Lenny Henry adopted Billie when she was just two weeks old. In an interview with in October 2021, the actress spoke about their unbreakable bond. "My biggest test and my biggest joy," she said of her child.
